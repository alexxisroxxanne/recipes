Ingredients:
	- (2 each) slice of good bread
	- (1 or 2 each) deli meat slice (turkey, ham, etc)
	- (1 or 2 each) slice of cheese (any kind)
	- (1 each) leaf of lettuce
	- (1 each) slice of red onion

Directions:
	1. Lightly toast both slices of bread
	2. Place meat on one slice of bread and cheese on other
	3. Toast meat-covered and cheese-covered bread in toaster oven
		until cheese is melted
	4. Place lettuce on top of meat and onion on top of cheese
	5. Press two sides together
	6. Cut in half
	7. Enjoy!
